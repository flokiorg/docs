## Getting Started

Once you've picked a version, the setup takes about 5 minutes:

1. **Download** the version for your operating system.
2. **Back up** your recovery phrase. Write it down and **never share it**.
3. **Sync** — let the wallet catch up with the network (a few minutes at most).
4. **Receive** — generate an address and get your first coins.
