## Quick Start (No App Store Needed)

You can install the Tap Wallet directly to your device in seconds. Open [taploki.com](https://taploki.com) in your browser and follow these steps:

### 🍎 iPhone (Safari)
1. Tap the **Share** icon (<Share2 size={18} inline />) at the bottom.
2. Scroll down and select **Add to Home Screen**.

### 🤖 Android (Chrome)
1. Tap the **Menu** (<MoreVertical size={18} inline />) icon at the top right.
2. Select **Install App** or **Add to Home Screen**.

### 💻 Desktop (Chrome/Edge)
1. Click the **Install** icon (<PlusSquare size={18} inline />) on the right side of the address bar.

## 🔗 One Wallet, Everywhere

Link your Flokicoin address to your social accounts to unlock cross-platform features. Your FLC stays synced whether you're on a laptop or your phone.

*   **Discord**: Join the [Flokicoin Discord](https://flokicoin.org/discord) and open the **App Launcher**.
*   **Telegram**: Launch via [@taplokiBot](https://flokicoin.org/telegram).
*   **Web**: Access anytime at [taploki.com](https://taploki.com).
