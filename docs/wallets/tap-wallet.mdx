## Quick Start (No App Store Needed)

You can install the Tap Wallet directly to your device in seconds. Open [taploki.com](https://taploki.com) in your browser and follow these steps:

### <Apple size={22} style={{verticalAlign: 'middle', marginRight: '8px'}} /> iPhone (Safari)
1. Tap the **Share** icon (<Share2 size={18} inline />) at the bottom.
2. Scroll down and select **Add to Home Screen**.

### <Bot size={22} style={{verticalAlign: 'middle', marginRight: '8px'}} /> Android (Chrome)
1. Tap the **Menu** (<MoreVertical size={18} inline />) icon at the top right.
2. Select **Install App** or **Add to Home Screen**.

### <Monitor size={22} style={{verticalAlign: 'middle', marginRight: '8px'}} /> Desktop (Chrome/Edge)
1. Click the **Install** icon (<PlusSquare size={18} inline />) on the right side of the address bar.

## <LinkIcon size={24} style={{verticalAlign: 'middle', marginRight: '8px'}} /> One Wallet, Everywhere

Link your Flokicoin address to your social accounts to unlock cross-platform features. Your FLC stays synced whether you're on a laptop or your phone.

*   **Discord**: Join the [Flokicoin Discord](https://flokicoin.org/discord) and open the **App Launcher**.
*   **Telegram**: Launch via [@taplokiBot](https://t.me/taplokibot).
*   **Web**: Access anytime at [taploki.com](https://taploki.com).

## <PartyPopper size={24} style={{verticalAlign: 'middle', marginRight: '8px'}} /> Tap Days

**Tap Days** are community-driven initiatives powered by miners and holders to let everyone experience the Flokicoin network firsthand. During these events, limited-time **Tap Codes** are dropped exclusively in our Discord and Telegram communities.

Every tap helps generate entry codes, allowing you to benefit from the network and reserve your seat for upcoming features. These codes have a strict time limit—once they drop, the clock starts ticking, so you'll need to claim yours quickly before they expire.

The entire initiative is 100% community-funded. Miners and contributors can fuel the tap directly through the Tap Wallet Mini App to keep it flowing for new users, unlocking exclusive **contributor roles and badges** on their connected social accounts as a mark of their support.
